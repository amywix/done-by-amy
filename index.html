FAQ

Common Questions

Can the chatbot match my brand?

Yes. The chatbot can use your colours, tone, services, questions and business details.

Can it capture leads?

Yes. It can collect names, phone numbers, email addresses, service types, locations and notes.

Can it connect to my systems?

Depending on the package, it can connect to tools like forms, email, spreadsheets, CRMs, calendars and automations.

Do I need a new website?

No. The chatbot can usually be added to an existing website. If your site needs improvement, a chatbot landing page can also be built.
